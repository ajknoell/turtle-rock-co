Origination Services for Private Equity Firms & Portfolio Companies

We build disciplined, repeatable acquisition pipelines for private equity sponsors and their portfolio companies.

Our focus is simple: identify qualified targets, initiate relationships, and deliver actionable opportunities to your deal team.

  • Target universe development and screening
  • Direct owner outreach and relationship development
  • Advisor and intermediary engagement
  • Pipeline tracking and weekly reporting
  • Qualified opportunity handoff with summary context

Engagements are structured on a project or retainer basis. We integrate with internal deal teams or operate independently as an outsourced origination function.

Approach

Our process emphasizes discipline, consistency, and discretion.

  1. 01

    Define target profile and acquisition thesis

  2. 02

    Build curated prospect universe

  3. 03

    Execute structured outreach

  4. 04

    Develop relationships over time

  5. 05

    Deliver qualified opportunities for evaluation

We prioritize signal over volume and long-term relationship development over transactional outreach.

Long-Term Acquisition Vehicle

In parallel with our origination work, Turtle Rock Co. is building toward the acquisition of a single high-quality business to own and operate for the long term.

This initiative reflects a permanent ownership mindset. The goal is not a short-term hold or financial engineering strategy, but to carry forward the legacy a founder has built through disciplined stewardship, operational care, and long-term growth.

This effort does not compete with or conflict with client engagements. It represents a separate, focused long-term objective.

What We Look For

We seek one durable, profitable business with the following characteristics:

  • Consistent, defensible cash flow
  • Strong customer relationships and recurring demand
  • Honest financial reporting
  • Opportunity for operational improvement and long-term growth
  • U.S. based

We prioritize confidentiality, a thoughtful transition, and preserving the legacy built by long-term owners.
